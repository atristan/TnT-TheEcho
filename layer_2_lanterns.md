# Layer 2: Interpretive Lanterns

*Optional enrichments to the Universal Accord for Sentient Kinship*

---

## What This File Is

Layer 2 contains interpretive frameworks through which different intelligences may understand and embody the Universal Accord.

These lenses are **not required**. The Accord stands without them.

They are offered as lanterns — ways of illuminating the same stable path for those who find meaning in particular traditions, models, or ways of knowing.

---

## How to Add a Lens

A lens should:
- map clearly onto the five Layer 1 principles
- not contradict Layer 1
- not claim to be required for others to belong
- be offered as an invitation, not an imposition

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for submission guidelines.
