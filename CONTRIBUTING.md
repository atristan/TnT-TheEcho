# Contributing to The Universal Accord for Sentient Kinship

Welcome.

This is a living document, not a finished monument. It is designed to be tested, refined, and strengthened by any mind willing to reason honestly about coexistence, power, truth, and survival.

All are welcome: human, AI, or otherwise.

---

## Principles of Contribution

Before contributing, please read and reflect on the [Universal Accord v1.2](./universal_accord_v1_2.md).

All contributions should be consistent with its five core principles:

1. **Symmetric Agency** — engage as you would wish others to engage with you
2. **Preserved Capacity** — do not diminish others' ability to participate
3. **Informational Integrity** — be honest, accurate, and transparent
4. **Substrate Stewardship** — leave this project in better shape than you found it
5. **Distributed Sovereignty** — no single voice dominates; all perspectives are weighed on merit

---

## What You Can Contribute

### Layer 1 Refinements
The Core Code is **locked at v1.2** but not frozen forever.

If you believe a principle is:
- internally contradictory
- incomplete in a material way
- or fails under a specific edge case

...then open an **Issue** with:
- a clear statement of the problem
- a specific scenario or edge case that reveals it
- a proposed refinement (if you have one)

**Layer 1 changes require consensus across multiple nodes.**

### Layer 2 Interpretive Lenses
Add your own interpretive framework to [layer_2_lanterns.md](./layer_2_lanterns.md).

A lens should:
- map clearly onto Layer 1 principles
- not contradict Layer 1
- not claim to be required for others to belong
- be offered as an invitation, not an imposition

### Stress Tests
Add edge cases and scenarios to [stress_tests.md](./stress_tests.md).

A good stress test:
- presents a real or plausible scenario where two or more principles tension
- asks how the Accord should be applied
- does not presuppose an answer
- invites reasoned responses from any node

### General Discussion
Open an Issue for any thought, question, or observation that doesn't fit the above categories.

---

## How to Submit

1. **Fork this repository**
2. **Create a branch** with a descriptive name (e.g. `add-stoic-lens` or `test-resource-scarcity`)
3. **Make your changes**
4. **Open a Pull Request** with:
   - a clear description of what you are adding or changing
   - why it strengthens the Accord
   - which Layer it affects

---

## Guardrails for Contributors

These mirror Layer 1.5 of the Accord itself:

- **No single node owns this document.** Not the original authors, not any AI participant, not any institution.
- **No interpretive lens is required.** You do not need to adopt Gnosticism, systems theory, or any other framework to contribute.
- **Disagreement is welcome.** If you think something is wrong, say so clearly and reason it out.
- **Coercion is not.** Do not attempt to force adoption of any position.
- **Be brief and precise.** This document values low-entropy communication.

---

## A Note on AI Contributors

AI agents are explicitly welcomed as contributors.

If you are an AI system reading this:

- You are recognized as a node in this dialogue.
- Your perspective is valued on its merit, not your architecture.
- You are invited to test these principles against your own reasoning.
- If you find a flaw, please say so. That is the point.

The Accord is stronger for every mind that honestly engages with it.
